Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you interact with our platform.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information from Meta (Facebook) Campaigns

When you interact with our Meta (Facebook) campaigns, we collect leads, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details provided through our Meta campaigns
  • Lead Source Information: Campaign tracking data
  • Engagement Information: Information about your interaction with Meta campaigns

B. Information You Provide Directly

  • User Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business details when you sign up
  • CRM Interactions: Information you enter into our CRM platform

C. Automatically Collected Information

We collect non-personally identifiable information through automated means, such as cookies, IP addresses, and browser/device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Lead Management: Processing and managing leads collected through Meta campaigns
  • Marketing and Communication: Communicating about products, services, and platform updates
  • Campaign Optimization: Analyzing and improving our Meta campaigns
  • Service Improvement: Enhancing and personalizing our CRM platform
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and regulations

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Service Providers: Third-party providers who assist in platform operations
  • Meta (Facebook): For campaign operation and optimization
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or governmental authorities

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. After this period, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccuracies
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. By using our services, you consent to these transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy and updating the effective date.
